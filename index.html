Máté Nagy 

Ph.D. Student, Developer

  I received my Master degree in Electrical Engineering from the
  Budapest University of Technology and Economics (BME) in 2010.
  In 2012 I joined the High-Speed Network Laboratory (HSN Lab) at
  BME as a Phd student. My research interests span network routing,
  SDN and data compression. At the same time I work as a senior
  developer in Ericsson Hungary.
